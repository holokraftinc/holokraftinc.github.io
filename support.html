How To

Follow these step-by-step instructions to get the most out of Noted.

How to Sign In to Noted

Noted uses Google Sign-In for secure authentication. You'll need a Google account to use the app.

1

Open the Noted app

Launch Noted from your Android home screen or app drawer. You'll see the welcome screen on first launch.

2

Tap "Continue with Google"

On the sign-in screen, tap the "Continue with Google" button. A Google account selector will appear.

3

Select your Google account

Choose the Google account you want to use with Noted. If you have multiple accounts, select the one you prefer for your Noted data.

4

Review and grant permissions

Review the permissions Noted is requesting (basic profile and email). Tap "Allow" to proceed.

5

You're in!

You'll be taken to your Noted home screen. Your account is created and cloud sync is active. Start adding tasks, notes, or reminders right away.

Tip: If you see a "Sign in failed" error, ensure your device has a stable internet connection and that your Google account is properly set up on your Android device (Settings → Accounts → Google).

How to Sync Google Calendar

Sync your Google Calendar to see events in the Noted Timeline. This is optional and you can revoke access at any time.

1

Open Settings in Noted

Tap the profile icon or menu icon at the top of the Noted home screen, then tap "Settings".

2

Find "Calendar Sync"

In Settings, scroll to the "Integrations" or "Calendar" section and tap "Google Calendar Sync".

3

Tap "Connect Google Calendar"

Tap the connect button. A Google account selector will appear, followed by a permissions screen.

4

Grant calendar read access

Review the permission — Noted only requests read-only access to view your calendar events. Tap "Allow" to proceed.

5

View your calendar in the Timeline

Return to the home screen and open the Timeline view. Your Google Calendar events will now appear alongside your tasks.

To disconnect: Go to Settings → Calendar Sync → Disconnect, or visit myaccount.google.com/permissions and remove Noted's access.

How to Enable Notifications

Noted sends local notifications for reminders and meeting alerts. Here's how to make sure you receive them.

1

Allow notification permission in-app

When you set your first reminder in Noted, the app will ask for notification permission. Tap "Allow" to enable notifications.

2

If you denied the permission

If you accidentally denied notifications, go to your Android Settings → Apps → Noted → Notifications, and toggle notifications on.

3

On Android 12+ — Exact Alarm permission

For precise reminder timing on Android 12 and later, Noted may ask for "Schedule Exact Alarms" permission. Grant this in Settings → Apps → Noted → Permissions to ensure reminders fire at exactly the right time.

4

Disable battery optimization for Noted

On some Android devices, battery optimization can prevent reminders from firing. Go to Settings → Battery → Battery Optimization → Noted → Don't Optimize for the most reliable notifications.

5

Test a reminder

Set a reminder in Noted for 1–2 minutes from now to verify notifications are working correctly on your device.

How to Restore Your Data

Your Noted data is backed up to the cloud automatically. Restoring it on a new device or after reinstalling is easy.

1

Install Noted on your new device

Download and install Noted - Day Planner from the Google Play Store on your new Android device.

2

Sign in with the same Google account

Open Noted and sign in with the exact same Google account you used previously. This is the key to restoring your data.

3

Wait for sync to complete

After signing in, Noted will automatically sync your tasks, notes, lists, and reminders from the cloud. This may take a few seconds to a minute depending on how much data you have.

4

Your data is restored

All your tasks, notes, smart lists, and settings will be restored and ready to use.

Important: You must sign in with the same Google account to restore your data. Data is account-specific and cannot be transferred between different Google accounts.

How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your Noted account at any time. This action is permanent and will remove all your data.

1

Open Noted Settings

In the Noted app, tap your profile icon or the menu, then navigate to "Settings".

2

Tap "Account"

In Settings, tap on the "Account" section to see account management options.

3

Tap "Delete Account"

Scroll to the bottom and tap "Delete Account". A confirmation dialog will appear.

4

Confirm deletion

Read the confirmation message carefully. Tap "Delete Permanently" to confirm. This action cannot be undone.

For full details on what gets deleted and the deletion timeline, see our dedicated Account Deletion page. You can also request deletion by emailing privacy@holokraftinc.com.

How to Report a Bug

Found a bug? We want to know about it. Good bug reports help us fix issues faster.

1

Note what happened

Write down what you were doing when the bug occurred, what you expected to happen, and what actually happened.

2

Include your device details

Note your device model, Android version (found in Settings → About Phone), and the version of Noted you are using (found in Noted Settings → About).

3

Send us a report

Email your bug report to support@holokraftinc.com with the subject line "Bug Report: [brief description]". Include screenshots or a screen recording if possible — they are extremely helpful.

4

We'll respond and fix it

We aim to acknowledge all bug reports within 2 business days and prioritize critical bugs for immediate fixes. We'll let you know when the fix is released.
