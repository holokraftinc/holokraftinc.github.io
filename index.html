Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Noted. Can't find your answer? Contact us.

Yes, Noted is completely free to download on Google Play. All core features — tasks, notes, smart lists, reminders, Google Calendar sync, and cloud sync — are available at no cost. We may introduce optional premium features in the future, but the core experience will always remain free.
Yes, Noted uses Google Sign-In for authentication. This lets us securely identify you and associate your data with your account for cloud sync. We only access your basic profile information (name and email) and, only if you choose, your Google Calendar data for event display.
No. Noted only reads your Google Calendar events to display them inside the app alongside your tasks and timeline. We never create, edit, delete, or modify any of your Google Calendar events. Your calendar data is read-only within Noted.
Yes! Noted stores your tasks, notes, and lists locally on your device. You can create, view, and edit all your data without an internet connection. When you reconnect to the internet, Noted automatically syncs your changes to the cloud, keeping everything up to date across your devices.
Your data is stored securely using Supabase, which uses row-level security to ensure only you can access your own data. All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections, and your account is protected by Google's OAuth 2.0 authentication — one of the industry's most secure authentication standards.
When you first set a reminder, Noted will ask for notification permission on Android. Grant the permission to receive on-time alerts. You can also go to your Android Settings → Apps → Noted → Notifications and enable them manually. Reminders work using local notifications, so they fire even without an internet connection.
Yes, absolutely. You can delete your account directly from within the Noted app under Settings → Account → Delete Account. You can also visit our Account Deletion page for detailed instructions. All your personal data — tasks, notes, lists, and reminders — will be permanently removed within 30 days of your deletion request.
Currently, Noted is available exclusively on Android. We are actively developing a web version and an iOS app. These will be announced on this website and through the Google Play listing. Sign up for updates or follow us on GitHub to stay informed about upcoming releases.
